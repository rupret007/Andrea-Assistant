- **Name:** German word meaning roughly "to fail" / "to fall through." Punk-coded naming.
- **Members (known):**

  - Lucky Ivy (Jeff's lifelong best friend — already in the band with Jeff in HS)
  - *(other members TBD — Jeff to add)*

- **Era:** high school (pre-Stalemate, so pre-1997).
- **Notes:** Predecessor to Stalemate. Foundation of Jeff + Lucky's musical partnership. TBD: any recordings, setlists, or documents surviving from the Durchfallen era.
