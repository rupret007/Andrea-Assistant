# Creative Sparks

Running log of ideas Jeff wants to revisit but not yet act on. Sparks are lightweight — don't overthink them. They can be songs, code, business, life, music-video concepts, anything that doesn't quite fit elsewhere.

## How they get here

- `/idea-capture` routes creative-but-not-urgent thoughts here
- Jeff explicitly says "save this as a spark"
- After conversations, Andrea offers "want me to spark that?" for interesting ideas that didn't become tasks

## Review cadence

The Saturday creative-block ritual surfaces sparks worth revisiting. So do monthly reviews. Sparks that keep resurfacing and feel alive → upgrade to a `/mission` or a proper task.
