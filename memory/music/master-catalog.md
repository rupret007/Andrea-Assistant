# Master Music Catalog

Canonical index of every Jeff Story musical work. Cross-references lyrics, demos, mixes, released versions, voice memos.

## Status vocab
- `fragment` — short idea, not yet structured
- `sketch` — verse+chorus structure, not recorded seriously
- `wip` — multi-track or multi-version exists
- `demo` — full recording, not final-mixed
- `mixed` — mixed but not released
- `released` — on streaming
- `lost-to-time` — mentioned once, can't locate

## Priority vocab
- `active` — working on it now
- `dormant` — untouched 3+ months
- `archived` — intentionally set aside
- `forgotten` — untouched 6+ months, worth re-look
